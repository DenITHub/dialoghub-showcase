# DialogHub

![DialogHub network visual](./assets/dialoghub-header.jpg)

DialogHub is an AI-native platform for business communication, lead capture,
and workflow automation.

It helps companies turn incoming conversations into structured workflows:
capture demand, qualify leads, prepare responses, coordinate follow-up, and
keep human control where it matters.

## What DialogHub Helps With

- Capture and qualify leads from customer conversations
- Connect business communication channels into one operating workspace
- Automate repetitive follow-up, notifications, and content workflows
- Review and approve sensitive AI-assisted actions before they go out
- Track usage, billing, integrations, and workspace setup from one place
- Build a practical operating layer for small and mid-sized businesses

## Product Areas

| Area | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| AI communication workspace | Manage customer conversations, leads, and review flows |
| Lead capture and qualification | Turn visitor/customer messages into structured lead records |
| Workflow automation | Route repetitive tasks into controlled business workflows |
| Human-in-the-loop review | Keep approval steps for replies, content, and sensitive actions |
| Integrations | Connect communication, billing, notification, and automation systems |
| Product operations | Monitor usage, plans, connected modules, and workspace state |
