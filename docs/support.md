# Support

For DialogHub support, contact:

- hello@dialoghub.cloud

Useful links:

- Website: https://dialoghub.cloud
- Platform: https://dialoghub.cloud/console

## What To Include

When asking for support, include:

- your workspace or account email
- the area you are using
- what you expected to happen
- what actually happened
- screenshots if the issue is visual

Do not send passwords, API keys, bot tokens, or other secrets in support
messages.
