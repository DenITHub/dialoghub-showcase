# Roadmap

This roadmap is intentionally public and high-level. It avoids internal
implementation details and may change as the product evolves.

## Now

- AI-assisted customer communication workspace
- Conversational lead capture and qualification
- Connected business communication modules
- Human review and approval flows
- Usage, billing, and workspace management

## Next

- More polished public product showcase
- More screenshots and guided product walkthroughs
- Stronger onboarding and activation flows
- Improved management views for connected modules
- Better reporting around leads, conversations, and automation outcomes

## Later

- More business workflow automation modules
- More integration options
- More analytics and operational reporting
- Voice and richer real-time interaction surfaces
- Deeper product templates for specific business types

## Roadmap Principle

DialogHub should stay practical: automation must reduce operational work,
preserve human control where needed, and create measurable business value.
