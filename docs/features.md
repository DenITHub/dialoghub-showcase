# Features

DialogHub is designed as an operating workspace for AI-assisted business
communication and automation.

## Communication Workspace

- Central place to manage incoming customer conversations
- Workspace-based setup and management flow
- Clear separation between onboarding, daily operation, and account management
- Human review paths for actions that should not be fully automatic

## Lead Capture and Qualification

- Conversational lead capture from customer messages
- Structured lead records for business follow-up
- Qualification signals such as intent, urgency, and fit
- Owner notifications when important leads appear

## AI-Assisted Responses

- AI-assisted customer communication
- Business-context-aware response generation
- Review-first flows for sensitive replies
- Human handoff when automation should pause

## Workflow Automation

- Automated follow-up and notification flows
- Content and research workflow support
- Event-driven automations around customer activity
- Roadmap path for deeper business-process automation

## Integrations and Operations

- Connected communication modules
- Usage and quota visibility
- Billing and subscription management
- Integration health and workspace state visibility

## Designed For

DialogHub is built for small and mid-sized businesses that need practical
automation without giving up control of customer-facing communication.
