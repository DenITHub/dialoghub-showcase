# Integrations

DialogHub is built around connected business workflows. Integrations may change
over time, so this document describes the integration categories rather than
locking the product to a fixed channel list.

## Communication

DialogHub connects customer communication surfaces into a managed workspace so
businesses can capture, respond, review, and follow up from one product flow.

## Automation

Workflow automation supports routing, notifications, scheduled tasks, and
follow-up processes around customer activity.

## AI Providers

DialogHub is designed to work with multiple AI model providers. Public product
copy avoids binding the platform to one specific model vendor.

## Billing and Plans

Subscription, usage, plan, and entitlement logic are part of the product
operating layer so customers can understand what is active and what is included.

## Knowledge and Business Context

DialogHub uses business context to make AI-assisted communication more useful,
more relevant, and easier to review.
